LinkedIn Optimization

  1. Go to your personal LinkedIn Profile page.
  2. Look for the floating Hirdlogic icon on the right side.
  3. Click the floating icon to open the optimization panel.

Job Tracker

  1. Go to any job listing page (LinkedIn, Indeed, Lever, Greenhouse, etc.)
  2. Hover or click the floating Hirdlogic icon on the right side.
  3. Save the job to your tracker and view more options.
